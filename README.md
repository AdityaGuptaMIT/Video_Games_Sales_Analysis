**🎮 Video Game Sales Analysis (1980–2024)**

An exploratory data analysis (EDA) mini project built with Python, analyzing global video game sales trends across genres, consoles, publishers, and regions from 1980 to 2024.

** Visualizations Include:**
- Top 10 genres and publishers by total global sales
- Annual sales trend (1990–2024)
- Top 10 gaming consoles by sales
- Regional sales distribution (NA, Europe, Japan, Other)
- Critic score distribution and its correlation with sales
- Sales spread across top genres (box plot)

** Tech Stack:**
- Python, Pandas, NumPy
- Matplotlib, Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

** Dataset:** Video Game Sales (1980–2024) — Raw CSV
